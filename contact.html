  • Map of the Lab's location

    Slide 1 of 5: A map of the labs location, starting from the parkinglot and heading towards the building.

  • Entrance to complex

    Slide 2 of 5: Entering through center of the parking lot, there is an oepn gate near a planter and a light pole. Enter through here.

  • Path to lab

    Slide 3 of 5: There is a winding path. Following the main forward and wide path, ignore the first two forks. The third will be a large fork between two building with a canopy and canopy poles.

  • Turn Left To Building 2

    Slide 4 of 5: At the canopy fork, turn left and soon there will be a widened path to the left of the canopy path. This is where Partnership 2 is.

  • Lab Room 108

    Slide 5 of 5: The first door in the building near the corner is ours. It is immediately right of a garage door and directly on the corner.
